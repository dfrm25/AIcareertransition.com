- Pillar: `https://aicareertransition.com/career.html`

## Outreach Targets

- Career coaches who publish job search resource lists.
- Edtech newsletters covering AI upskilling.
- Productivity newsletters covering ChatGPT/Copilot workflows.
- Bootcamp and course directories with free resource pages.
- HR/recruiting blogs writing about AI skills on resumes.
- Podcasts or newsletters interviewing career changers.

## Weekly Quota

- 20 qualified prospects per week.
- 10 personalized outreach emails or DMs per week.
- 2 guest post or quote pitches per week.
- 1 LinkedIn thread repurposed from a cluster article per week.

## Pitch Angles

- "Free AI career transition roadmap for non-technical professionals."

- "30-60-90 day plan for moving into AI-adjacent work."
