ChatGPT prompts for product managers and adjacent roles (and how to know if you are ready)

If you ship roadmaps, write PRDs, and translate customer pain into specs, the right AI prompts cut prep time. This page points to product-focused prompts in our library, plus a short path to test your AI readiness.

Product prompts in our library

The Product section includes prompts for user stories, discovery questions, stakeholder updates, and crisp PRD drafts. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, fill the brackets, and edit the output before it ships.

AI readiness for product teams

Readiness is not about buzzwords. It is whether you can choose a model, write a decent prompt, and sanity-check outputs before they go to legal or leadership. Our free eight-question quiz on the homepage places you on a path: fundamentals (AI 101), heavy prompt use (library + blog), or advanced automation (AI 201).
