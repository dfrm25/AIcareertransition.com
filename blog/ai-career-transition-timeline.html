30-60-90 Day AI Career Transition Timeline

A 90-day plan will not make you an AI researcher. It can make you credible in one AI-adjacent work lane if you focus.

Direct answer

Use the first 30 days to choose a lane and learn the basics, days 31-60 to build one proof artifact, and days 61-90 to turn that proof into resume bullets, LinkedIn copy, interview stories, and targeted applications.

Days 1-30: focus and foundations

  • Pick one role-adjacent lane from the AI career transition roadmap.
  • Finish the relevant parts of AI 101.
  • Choose one repeated task you can improve with AI.
  • Keep a decision log: what worked, what failed, what needed human review.

Days 31-60: build proof

Build one case study, not six half-finished demos. Use a real workflow if possible. If you cannot use work data, use public or anonymized sample data and clearly label it.

Start with AI career artifacts and the portfolio examples.

Days 61-90: package and apply

Rewrite your resume around evidence. Update LinkedIn with one clear AI-assisted workflow. Prepare interview stories using the structure: situation, workflow, tool, verification, result, lesson.

Use AI interview prep and AI skills on a resume to avoid inflated claims.

Keep the scope narrow

Your 90-day result should be one credible lane, one artifact, and one story. That is enough to start conversations.
